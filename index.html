About DSSYWC

DSSYWC is the annual flagship event of IEEE India Council which administers a platform to young professionals, academicians, scholars, entrepreneurs and technocrats from divergent fields and careers to show up together and share a common podium. The Congress provides a path to connect with industry experts, successful business professionals and experts working on latest technologies.

Various workshops, seminars led by ace professionals contribute to the diverse opportunities for business networking, professional development and personal growth.
